My Love for Dune

Four years ago, I went to Barnes and Noble in search for a science fiction book, and I came across Dune, I gave it a read and since then, it's been of my favorite books of all time. When I heard that a film was coming up for it, I was ecstatic, and couldn't wait for it to release. And now that it's been released for about four years already, it has become one of my top favorite movies.

The Reasons Why I Love Dune

  1. The Worldbuilding
    • The Fremen culture
    • The Arrakis enviroment and life culture
    • The Harkonennen culture
  2. The Characters
    • Paul Atreides (the goat)
    • Duncan Idaho (second goat)
    • Stilgard
  3. The Themes
    • Environment
    • Politics
    • Religion
